The Knowledge Training Academy is just starting its journey, but Roy Baxendell is bringing over 35years experience and knowledge to the field of logistics. Roy Baxendell started his time as a field service engineer for Hotpoint in 1985. Serving them for 30years with the final 5 years as a training instructor.

He continued developing his skills at AO.com and the latter NGC Logistics specialising in customer services and white goods installation training . His natural skill of taking a concept and designing and producing professional documentation, such as training booklets or quick guides complimented these businesses.

In his time at NGC Logistics, he produced workshops for Heavy & Bulky that were endorsed by Amazon that ensured the delivery associates working on behalf of Amazon could take these newfound skills to the customers in the 2 man delivery service.
