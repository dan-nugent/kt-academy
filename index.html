Manual Handling

This Manual Handling Training Workshop helps ensure those attending are sufficiently trained in the principles and practices of safe manual handling.

This workshop provides the necessary information and training for employees and contractors to understand more about the risks associated with manual handling, how to undergo a risk assessment and ensure appropriate control measures are put in place.

Who is it for:

Any person wishing to progress their learning in effective manual handling techniques for the safety of them and others.

Entry Requirements

There are no formal entry requirement for this workshop.

Key Features:

  • Give learners knowledge of Manual Handling techniques to support them.
  • Covers all areas of Health & Safety to eliminate injuries.
  • Complete risk assessments when Manual Handling any goods small or large.
  • On completion the learner will receive a certificate of completion.
